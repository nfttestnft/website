What are the Orcs of Orakion?

The Orcs of Orakion are a collection of programmatically, randomly generated NFTs on the Ethereum blockchain. They consist of 10,101 randomly created Orcs with over 500 million possible trait combinations.

The orcs have different backgrounds, faces, hair, ears, armor, necklaces, weapons, scars, wounds, tattoos and warpaint. Some traits are rarer than others but all Orcs stick together and venture into the unknown world as one uniform group!

One tribe, one adventure!

The orcs are fleeing the cursed land of Orakion and seek new adventures in an unknown world. Each Orc holder can join our community and experience interactive communtiy events full of wonder, epic tales, Pen & Paper like adventures and loot!

About every 2 weeks we will hold live CEs in Discord. We will screen a new Episode of our adventure story with full audio production. The community can decide the turns of the story and guide the Orcs through their adventure by voting on decisions!

The decisions change the story, impact future storylines and decide whether loot is won or overlooked!

Being an Orc of Orakion means experiencing a unique adventure together with friends and likeminded people. We would like to make this adventure even more special by gifting you long lasting memories in form of NFTs eternalized on the Ethereum Blockchain.

→ We plan to have artists illustrate a scene of each Episode/CE and turn it into a limited NFT, which can be minted by each Orc for free!

→ While venturing into the unknown world our Orcs will eventually meet new races. We will create new Avatar NFTs for each race and offer them free to mint (+gas) to all Orc holders: See Roadmap!

Members who participate in our CEs can take part in raffles/give-aways and get a chance to win loot from our treasure chamber in form of NFTs. These can be lost or captured Orcs or NFTs form other projects that we buy with earnings from our royalties or receive through collaborations!

The treasure chamber is the store for NFTs for give-aways that will be gifted during CEs. We will fuel it by collaborations and dedicating a certain percentage of our royalties to it.

The Vault: We will use our own funds as well as royalties to rescue Orcs that got lost in the wild as well as use assets and funds to build a sustainable business.

We plan to build a fantasy world and platform for fantasy fans that is second to none. This will include fans of art, gaming, boardgames and many more.

5.1 NFT-Forge

Produce our own art collection! This could also enable collaborations with amazing artists or NFT-communities!

5.2 NFT-Incubator

Build a fundraising platform or forum for aspiring artists. Discover new talents and fund your favorite artist or art-project. Receive personalized perks, NFTs and or a cut of the artists royalties in exchange.

5.3 Pen & Paper adventure!

We could start an interactive Pen & Paper adventure similar to the adventure/story mode of the Community Events, but with representatives of the community playing the complex Pen & Paper Events!

For more information please visit our Discord!
