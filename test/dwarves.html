What are the Dwarves?

The Dwarves are a collection of programmatically, randomly generated NFTs on the Ethereum Blockchain with a high resolution of 5000 x 5000 pixel and 500 million possible trait combinations.

The dwarves have different backgrounds, beards, hair, ears, armor, runes, weapons, scars, wounds, tattoos and warpaint. Some traits are rarer than others.
