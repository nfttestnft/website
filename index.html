What are the Orcs of Orakion?

The Orcs of Orakion are a collection of programmatically, randomly generated NFTs on the Ethereum blockchain. They consist of 10,101 randomly created Orcs with over 100 million possible trait combinations.

The orcs have different backgrounds, faces, hair, ears, armor, necklaces, weapons, scars, wounds, tattoos and warpaint. Some traits are rarer than others but all Orcs stick together and venture into the unknown world as one uniform group!

What are the Orcs doing?

The orcs are fleeing the cursed land of Orakion and seek new adventures in an unknown world. Each Orc holder can join our community and experience interactive communtiy events full of wonder, epic tales, D&D like adventures and loot!

About every 2 weeks we will hold live CEs in Discord. We will screen a new Episode of our adventure story with full audio production. The community can decide the turns of the story and guide the Orcs through their adventure by voting on decisions!

The decisions change the story, impact future storylines and decide wether loot is won or overlooked!

Being an Orc of Orakion means experiencing a unique adventure together with friends and likeminded people. We would like to make this adventure even more special by gifting you long lasting memories in form of NFTs eternalized on the Ethereum Blockchain.

→ We plan to have artists illustrate a scene of each Episode/CE and turn it into a limited NFT, which can be minted by each Orc for free!

Members who participate in our CEs can take part in raffles/give-aways and get a chance to win loot from our treasure chamber in form of NFTs. These can be lost or captured Orcs or NFTs form other projects that we buy with earnings from our royalties or receive through collaborations!

The treasure chamber is the store for NFTs for give-aways that will be gifted during CEs. We will fuel it by collaborations and dedicating a certain percentage of our royalties to it.

The Vault: We will use our own funds as well as royalties to rescue Orcs that got lost in the wild as well as use assets and funds to build a sustainable buisiness.

We plan to build a fantasy world and platform for fantasy fans that is second to none. This will include fans of art, gaming, boardgames and many more.

FAQ

What’s an NFT?

An NFT stands for “Non-fungible token” and is a fancy way of saying it’s a unique, one of a kind digital item that users can buy, own, and trade. Some NFTs main function are to be digital art and look cool, some offer additional utility like exclusive access to websites or participation in an event, think of it like a rare piece of art that can also act as a “members” card.

What is Metamask? Metamask is a crypto wallet that can store your Ethereum, and is needed to purchase and mint a Cool Cat. Having a wallet gives you an Ethereum address (i.e. 0xABCD….1234), this is where your NFT will be stored. Learn more about Metamask and how easy it is to use over here! (https://metamask.io/)

Where does my NFT go after I purchase an Orc of Orakion?

Your ruff Orc NFT will appear in whatever address, or connected wallet you used to purchase the Orc of Orakion. You can see your Orcs of Orakion here from our website, by clicking My Orcs, or view them on Opensea. (Link to Opensea)
