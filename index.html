What are the Orcs of Orakion?

We are building a fantasy community that brings together artists, writers, fantasy fans and NFT collectors. Together we build a creative hub where fantasy fans from all over the world come together and co-create an epic world and adventure:
→ Form alliances & friendships, engage, write, design and shape the story with your creativity!

The Orcs of Orakion are a collection of programmatically, randomly generated NFTs on the Ethereum Blockchain with a high resolution of 5000 x 5000 pixel and 500 million possible trait combinations.

The orcs have different backgrounds, faces, hair, ears, armor, necklaces, weapons, scars, wounds, tattoos and warpaint. Some traits are rarer than others but all Orcs stick together and venture into the unknown world as one uniform group!
